Networking Terms

bridge
an aggregation of two or more networks (usually layer-2 networks)
tunneling
moving data from one network to another by repackaging the packets
link aggregation (a.k.a. bonding)
an aggregation of two or more network connections (usually interfaces), providing connection load balancing and/or redundancy.
